# StepReceipt Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 11, 2026

StepReceipt is a personal Apple Health activity dashboard for iPhone.

## Health Data

StepReceipt requests read-only Apple Health access for steps, walking and running distance, active energy, flights climbed, and workouts. This data is used to show your daily timeline, activity history, goals, insights, share cards, and optional household competition totals.

StepReceipt does not write workouts or health samples in this version.

## On-Device Processing

Raw Apple Health samples, hourly buckets, individual workout details, workout source identifiers, and workout source names stay on your iPhone.

## iCloud Sync

If iCloud is available and you use sync features, StepReceipt may store aggregate daily summaries, goals, preferences, and optional aggregate household competition rows using Apple's CloudKit service.

The household competition board stores aggregate activity totals linked to a household invite code. It does not upload raw Apple Health samples or individual workout details.

## Tracking and Advertising

StepReceipt does not track you across apps or websites. StepReceipt does not include third-party advertising.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the feedback email provided in TestFlight or App Store Connect.
